| Monitoring | Explosion early warning systems are used in various settings, including industrial facilities, military zones, urban environments, and disaster-prone areas. For example, the Explosion Early Warning System (EWS) used in the oil and gas industry is equipped with sensors such as gas, flow, and temperature sensors. It aims to effectively identify potential hazard zones and provide early warnings to nearby workers and industrial safety monitoring teams. Through real‑time monitoring and data analysis, the system optimises safety protocols and prevents casualties and asset damage caused by gas leaks. This approach enhances worker safety in modern industrial environments and contributes to the protection of critical energy resources (Padmaa et al, 2024). | Eksplozijų ankstyvo įspėjimo sistemos naudojamos įvairiuose kontekstuose, įskaitant industriinius įrenginius, kariuotinus zonas, miesto aplinką ir katastrofų atpažįstamos erdvės. Pavyzdžiui, naftos ir dujų industrijose naudojama Eksplozijų ankstyvo įspėjimo sistema (EWS), kuri yra įrengta su dujų, srautas ir temperatūros sensorais.
